Multi-Scale Feature Fusion with Adaptive Attention for Robust Image Deblurring

Research Paper

MSFAA is a deep learning framework for deblurring images with spatially varying blur using multi-scale feature fusion and adaptive attention. It introduces the CAFI module to focus computation on heavily blurred regions, achieving +2.1 dB PSNR, +0.043 SSIM, and 18% lower compute cost over SOTA methods (IEEE EUROCON 2025).
